Robotics

It's very hard to document any robotics projects on the website short of uploading demo videos of them. So I'm going to use this space as a log of all past and current robotics projects...

TI-RSLK Robot

A maze traversing robot, this project was built as part of a kit made by Texas Instruments. It used a MSP430 with several bump switches. When it hit a wall it would back up and try a different angle and go again.

Autonomous Underwater submarine

This was part of a project and ultimately ended up failing, however, I learned alot about material science and structural integrity.

Range Finder

This used an ultrasonic sensor, a back-lit lcd screen, and a Potentiometer to control power to the screen. This was all processed on a Arduino. The way it worked was quite simple, the ultrasonic sensor sends out pings and the delay between sent and received determines the distance between it and any object its pointed at.

Object Tracking Bot

This robot was by far one of my most advanced as it relied on several peripherals to decide where to go. I used a mix between IR and Ultrasonic sensor as my robots eyes. I have a video of this bot handy so take a look!

Power Shutoff via water detection

This is my Senior Design project. It's a device that sits inside a pad-mounted ground transformer and when water rises to its sensors height, sends a wireless signal to a receiver on the switch that shuts it off. For this project I'm in charge of the microcontrollers and wireless communicaton. I've opted to use LoRaWan for communication and therefore selected the Arduino MKR 1310 as choice microcontroller.
